Environmentalism

 The environment has a deep impact on me, as it is what allows us to live, survive, and thrive on this planet. Seeing how the environment has been deteriorating due to climate change and resource exploitation has been disheartening to me since the environment is by far the most important thing to me. I want my future to help, directly or indirectly, the environment.
